Human Cell Tower

Has the ability to create a cellphone service region anywhere she desires, with herself at the center.

About

On a map, a new cellular region with limited radius would automatically appear, be registered to the govt cell tower database, and compatible to whatever cellular carrier desired. This way he/she can help both himself and others nearby in emergencies. Victims trapped under tunnels, in broken elevators, lost at sea, in a potentially hijacked commercial flight, etc. Can contact whoever they want. Caveat: Best used in absolute dead zones and remote regions. If power is activated in an actively registered cell, the overlapping region between that and the hero’s cell becomes a dead zone. Visual: Power activated - People trapped in an elevator are able to contact emergency services. However, calls drop from users located outside the elevator but within hero + cell tower radius.
