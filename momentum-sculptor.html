The Momentum-Sculptor

Martial artist who has the ability to absorb, store, and dish out external forces and momentum.

About this character

Trained in taiji, tae kwon do, traditional kung fu. Small battle scar under his right eye flares up when energy is being stored. He's patient, and never wastes motion. Displays extreme mental discipline and fortitude. Often refuses to fight unless absolutely necessary, as power can build dangerously and cause him to lose control. Stored power must be dished out within 5 minutes, otherwise it's prone to overload and cause self-harm.

Every punch blocked, every fall absorbed > "stored". This causes subtle changes in physical appearance. Like "I felt that ... and kept it." Sometimes he can't help letting out a slight smirk knowing how hard his next strike is gonna hit.
